# Meta Ads Campaign Template - NC Triangle

## Campaign Overview
**Objective**: Generate qualified life insurance leads in Triangle region
**Budget**: Start with $20/day, scale based on performance
**Duration**: 90 days initial test

## Target Audience
### Primary
- **Location**: Raleigh-Durham-Chapel Hill DMA
- **Age**: 25-45
- **Gender**: All
- **Interests**: Parenting, home buying, financial planning
- **Behaviors**: New parents, recent home buyers, engaged shoppers

### Secondary
- **Age**: 45-55 (pre-retirement)
- **Interests**: Retirement planning, investment
- **Income**: $50k-$150k household

## Ad Creative Templates

### Ad 1: Life Insurance Cost Calculator
**Headline**: "What Does $500k Life Insurance Actually Cost?"
**Primary Text**: 
"I built this calculator after 3 years in the industry. Most Triangle families are shocked by how affordable term life insurance actually is. 

✅ Takes 2 minutes
✅ Real rates from industry data 
✅ No sales pitch, just numbers

See your personalized cost breakdown - you might be surprised."

**Call-to-Action**: "Learn More"

### Ad 2: Middle-Class Financial Planning
**Headline**: "Financial Planning Isn't Just For The 1%"
**Primary Text**:
"I grew up thinking financial planning was for wealthy people. Turns out, the industry just makes it feel that way. 

These calculators use the same data the pros use, but without the gatekeeping. Perfect for Triangle families who want real numbers."

**Call-to-Action**: "Try Calculator"

### Ad 3: Local Triangle Focus
**Headline**: "Triangle Families: See Your Real Insurance Costs"
**Primary Text**:
"Raleigh-Durham-Chapel Hill area: Compare life insurance options with actual market rates. Built by someone who understands our local market.

Perfect for:
- New homeowners
- Growing families  
- Anyone who wants honest numbers"

**Call-to-Action**: "Calculate Now"

## Landing Page Copy
**Headline**: "Financial Tools for Triangle Families"
**Subheadline**: "I'm a Triangle professional with 3+ years experience who believes financial planning shouldn't be out of reach for middle-class families."

**Key Points**:
- Free to use
- Real industry data
- No sales pressure
- Designed for NC families
- Takes 2 minutes

## Budget Allocation
- **Life Insurance Calculator**: 60% of budget ($12/day)
- **Middle-Class Message**: 25% of budget ($5/day)  
- **Local Triangle Focus**: 15% of budget ($3/day)

## Performance Targets
- **CTR**: >2%
- **CPC**: <$2.50
- **Conversion Rate**: >10% (email capture)
- **Cost per Lead**: <$25
- **Monthly Leads**: 50-100

## Testing Plan
**Week 1**: Launch all 3 ad variations
**Week 2**: Optimize based on CTR/CPC
**Week 3**: Focus budget on best performer
**Week 4**: A/B test landing page headlines
**Month 2**: Scale winning combination
**Month 3**: Expand to additional calculators

## Tracking Setup
- Facebook Pixel on landing pages
- Google Analytics goals
- Email capture conversion tracking
- Lead quality scoring in CRM
