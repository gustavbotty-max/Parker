# Financial Calculators Hub - Lead Magnet Strategy

## Overview
Position Jonathan Parker as the go-to financial educator in NC/VA through a suite of free, valuable calculators that generate qualified leads.

## Target Audience
- **Primary**: Parents 25-45, homeowners, middle-class families in NC Triangle (Raleigh-Durham-Chapel Hill)
- **Secondary**: Young professionals 22-35 starting families/careers in Wake, Durham, Orange counties
- **Tertiary**: Pre-retirees 45-55 planning for the future in Triangle area

## Calculator Suite & Lead Flow

### 1. Life Insurance Calculator (LIVE)
**Purpose**: Immediate need recognition, high conversion
**Target**: Young families, new parents, homeowners
**Lead Strategy**: 
- "Email your personalized results"
- "Schedule a 15-minute review of your numbers"
- Follow-up sequence: education → consultation → close

### 2. Retirement Planning Calculator (Phase 2)
**Purpose**: Long-term planning, higher value prospects
**Target**: 30-50 year olds with established careers
**Lead Strategy**:
- "Get your retirement readiness score"
- "Free retirement planning checklist"
- Consultation for complex scenarios

### 3. College Savings Calculator (Phase 2)
**Purpose**: Young parent engagement, early relationship building
**Target**: Parents with kids under 10
**Lead Strategy**:
- "529 plan setup guide"
- "College savings action plan"
- Education about savings options

### 4. Debt Payoff Calculator (Phase 2)
**Purpose**: Problem-solving, immediate value provision
**Target**: People struggling with debt, high-interest loans
**Lead Strategy**:
- "Debt freedom roadmap"
- "Smart debt consolidation options"
- Financial coaching consultation

### 5. Emergency Fund Calculator (Phase 2)
**Purpose**: Basic financial literacy, broad appeal
**Target**: Everyone (broad net)
**Lead Strategy**:
- "Emergency fund starter kit"
- "High-yield savings account guide"
- Introduction to financial planning

## Marketing Funnel Strategy

### Top of Funnel (Awareness)
**Channels**:
- Meta/Facebook ads targeting local parents
- Google Ads for "life insurance calculator [city]"
- YouTube educational videos
- Local Facebook groups/community pages

**Ad Copy Examples**:
- "What does $500k of life insurance actually cost? Find out in 30 seconds"
- "Most families are underinsured by 40% - are you? Calculate your needs"
- "Free calculator: Compare all 5 types of life insurance side-by-side"

### Middle of Funnel (Engagement)
**Content Strategy**:
- Blog posts: "5 Signs You Need More Life Insurance"
- Videos: "Term vs Whole Life Insurance Explained"
- Infographics: "Life Insurance Needs by Age"
- Email series: "Financial Planning Basics"

### Bottom of Funnel (Conversion)
**Lead Magnets**:
- Personalized calculator results
- "Life Insurance Buyer’s Guide" PDF
- "Financial Protection Checklist"
- Free 15-minute consultation
