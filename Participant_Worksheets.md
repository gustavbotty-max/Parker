## REMEMBER THE GOLDEN RULE

**AUTOMATE IT!**

The moment you free up cash from any source, set up automatic transfer:
1. To debt (highest interest first)
2. To emergency fund (until you have 3-6 months)
3. To investment account

If you don't automate it, lifestyle creep will eat it.
