# 21 SOURCES OF CASH - Speaker Notes & Presentation Guide

---

### PRESENTATION OVERVIEW
- **Total Time:** 60-75 minutes
- **Target Audience:** Middle-class families, young parents, homeowners
- **Goal:** Educate on finding hidden cash + introduce business opportunity
- **Tone:** Empowering, not shaming; collaborative, not salesy

---

## SECTION 1: OPENING (5 minutes)

### SLIDE 1: Title Slide
**"21 Sources of Cash"**

**Talking Points:**
- "This is our first time running this as a group — excited you're here"
- "Collaborative group of advisors and business owners"
- "Mission: Help middle-class families with money"

**Energy:** Warm, welcoming, enthusiastic

**Ron Weber + The Origin**

---

## OBJECTIONS & RESPONSES

### "I don't make enough money"
**Response:** "Wealth is about keeping more, not making more. That's what these 21 sources are about — finding the money you're already earning but losing to inefficiencies."

### "I'll do it later"
**Response:** "Inflation is the boiling frog — it's cooking you now. Every month you wait, you're losing money to leaks you could fix today."

### "It's too complicated"
**Response:** "Start with ONE source. Master it. Then add another. You don't have to do all 21 this week."

## ENGAGEMENT TECHNIQUES

1. **Ask questions frequently:** "How many of you...?" "Who has...?"
2. **Pause after key stats:** Let numbers sink in
3. **Share personal stories:** "I fell into this trap..."
4. **Use "you" language:** Make it personal
5. **End sections with action:** "What will YOU do with this?"
