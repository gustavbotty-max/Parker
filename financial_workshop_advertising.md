## Key Messaging Tips for Each Platform

### Reddit Posts
- Lead with value: "Free Financial Workshop for Triangle Families"
- Include specific benefits: "Learn about life insurance, retirement planning, college savings"
- Mention it's free and local
- Use appropriate subreddit formatting (check community rules first)
- Be prepared to answer questions in comments

### Facebook Groups
- Use engaging images (if allowed)
- Share your personal story as a Primerica rep
- Focus on community benefit, not sales pitch
- Tag relevant local hashtags: #TriangleNC #RaleighNC #DurhamNC #ChapelHill

### Craigslist
- Keep it concise but informative
- Include date, time, location prominently
- Mention "free" multiple times
- Use clear subject line
